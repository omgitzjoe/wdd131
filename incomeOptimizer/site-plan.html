Overview

Purpose

To provide the information needed to make an informed decision for whether to cap income for benefits purposes and whether to use actual vs standard deduction for taxes. This sight is designed specifically for the 'gig' worker trying to make ends meet.

Audience

Those filing their own taxes and those close to the Medicaid income limits

Dynamic elements

Will take in user inputs of income, expenses, what type of work, and mileage percentage for work. It will then calculate and display your results.
